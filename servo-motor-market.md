<p><p>A servo motor is a highly specialized type of motor used for precise control of angular or linear position, velocity, and acceleration. It consists of a motor combined with a feedback mechanism to ensure accurate and consistent movement. Servo motors are widely used in applications such as robotics, CNC machinery, conveyor systems, and automation processes due to their reliability and efficiency.</p><p>The Servo Motor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand across various industries including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace. The growth is further fueled by advancements in technology and the rising trend of automation which necessitates the use of high-performance motors for enhanced production efficiency.</p><p>Latest trends in the market include the growing adoption of electric servo motors over hydraulic and pneumatic options due to their energy efficiency and reduced maintenance costs. Additionally, the integration of IoT and smart technologies is transforming servo motor applications, facilitating real-time monitoring and control. As manufacturing processes become increasingly sophisticated, the demand for servo motors that offer higher precision and adaptability is projected to soar, further propelling market growth.</p></p>
