<p><p>The Commercial UPS Power Supply market is primarily divided into two types: DC UPS and AC UPS. DC UPS systems provide direct current power, ideal for applications requiring stable voltage, such as telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, AC UPS units convert stored energy into alternating current, suitable for general electronic equipment and appliances. Both types ensure uninterrupted power supply, protecting critical infrastructure from outages and fluctuations, while enhancing operational reliability across various industries, including IT, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
