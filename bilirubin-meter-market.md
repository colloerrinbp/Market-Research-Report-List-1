<p><p>The bilirubin meter market is segmented into bench-top and transcutaneous types. Bench-top bilirubin meters are laboratory devices that provide precise measurements of bilirubin levels through blood samples, making them ideal for clinical settings. In contrast, transcutaneous bilirubin meters utilize a non-invasive approach, measuring bilirubin levels through the skin. This method offers quick and convenient screening, particularly in neonatal care. Both types are essential for diagnosing jaundice and monitoring bilirubin levels effectively in different healthcare environments.</p></p>
