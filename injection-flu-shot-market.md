<p><p>The injection flu shot market is primarily categorized into two types: trivalent and quadrivalent influenza vaccines. In contrast, the quadrivalent influenza vaccine offers broader protection by targeting four strains, including two A strains and two B strains. The quadrivalent option is increasingly preferred due to its enhanced coverage, aiming to reduce the risk of flu outbreaks and improve public health outcomes.</p></p>
