<p><strong>The Railway Gearboxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Drive</li><li>Transversal Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway gearboxes market is primarily categorized into longitudinal and transversal drive systems. Longitudinal drive gearboxes are designed to transmit power along the length of the train, enhancing traction and efficiency for high-speed applications. In contrast, transversal drive gearboxes facilitate power distribution across the width of the train, optimizing performance in urban and light rail systems. Both types are essential for improving train performance, reliability, and overall operational efficiency, catering to specific railway applications and design requirements.</p></p>
