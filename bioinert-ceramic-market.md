<p><p>The bioinert ceramic market primarily includes aluminum oxide (Al2O3) and zirconium oxide (ZrO2). Al2O3 is known for its excellent biocompatibility, mechanical strength, and wear resistance, making it ideal for dental and orthopedic implants. ZrO2, on the other hand, offers high toughness and aesthetic appeal, often used in dental crowns and bridges. Both materials are essential in medical applications due to their stability in biological environments, promoting enhanced integration with living tissues and minimizing rejection rates.</p></p>
