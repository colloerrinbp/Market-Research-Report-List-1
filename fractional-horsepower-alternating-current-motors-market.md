<p><p>Fractional Horsepower Alternating Current Motors comprise various types, including split phase motors, shaded pole motors, and reluctance motors. Split phase motors provide high starting torque, making them suitable for applications like fans and pumps. Shaded pole motors are simpler and cost-effective, commonly used in low-power applications such as small appliances. Reluctance motors operate based on variable reluctance and are often utilized in applications requiring precise control and efficiency. Each type addresses specific needs within various industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
