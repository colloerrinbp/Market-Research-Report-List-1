<p><p>The arc welding robots market is divided into two primary types: non-consumable electrode arc welding robots and consumable electrode arc welding robots. Non-consumable electrode robots utilize a permanent electrode that does not get consumed during the welding process, making them suitable for applications requiring precision and control. In contrast, consumable electrode arc welding robots utilize electrodes that melt and become part of the weld, allowing for faster production rates and versatility in various welding applications. Both types cater to different industrial needs.</p></p>
