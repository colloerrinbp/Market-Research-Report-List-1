CBL furnaces optimize heat transfer and feed conversion, while SRT furnaces focus on enhancing production efficiency through reduced residence times. USC furnaces aim for higher olefin yields by operating under extreme conditions. Each type has unique advantages and is tailored to specific production needs in the petrochemical industry.</p></p>
