<p><ul><li>Continuous Multi End Roving</li><li>Chopped Multi End Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass multi-end roving market is categorized into two primary types: Continuous Multi-End Roving and Chopped Multi-End Roving. Continuous Multi-End Roving consists of long strands used in processes like filament winding and pultrusion, offering high strength and durability for various applications. Chopped Multi-End Roving, on the other hand, involves shorter, randomly cut strands designed for easier handling and integration into composite materials. Both types serve essential roles in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace, enhancing product performance and versatility.</p></p>
