<p><p>The SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared) equipment market features two primary types: area scan products and line scan products. Area scan products capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed imaging of stationary objects, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, line scan products capture images one line at a time, ideal for inspecting moving objects, like conveyor belts, allowing for continuous monitoring and high-speed processing in areas like logistics and sorting.</p></p>
