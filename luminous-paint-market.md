<p><strong>The Luminous Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luminous paint market comprises three primary types: fluorescent, phosphorescent, and radio luminescent paints. Fluorescent paint absorbs UV light and emits a bright glow, ideal for vibrant, short-term applications. Phosphorescent paint stores light energy and releases it slowly, providing a lasting glow in the dark for extended periods. Radio luminescent paint incorporates radioactive materials to emit light, typically used in specialized applications like watch dials and emergency signs. Each type serves unique purposes across various industries, enhancing visibility and safety.</p></p>
