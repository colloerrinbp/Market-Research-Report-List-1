<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color print film market includes two main types: solvent-based films and water-based films. Solvent-based films utilize organic solvents for ink adhesion, offering vibrant colors and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based films rely on water as a primary solvent, resulting in less environmental impact and safer handling, but may have slightly less color vibrancy. Both types cater to various printing needs, with their distinct properties influencing their applications in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
