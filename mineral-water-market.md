<p><p>The mineral water market is primarily divided into two types: natural mineral water and man-made mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from mineral springs, containing dissolved minerals and trace elements, valued for its health benefits. In contrast, man-made mineral water is produced by artificially adding minerals to purified water, allowing for controlled mineral content. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers, but natural mineral water often has a premium appeal due to its origin and perceived purity.</p></p>
