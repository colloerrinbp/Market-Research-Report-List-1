<p><p>The Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) camera market is divided into cooled and uncooled categories. Cooled MWIR cameras utilize refrigerants or thermoelectric coolers to lower sensor temperatures, enhancing sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for applications like military surveillance and industrial monitoring. Uncooled MWIR cameras, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, offering a cost-effective solution with simpler design and lower maintenance needs, suitable for applications like building inspections and security. Each type serves distinct operational requirements.</p></p>
