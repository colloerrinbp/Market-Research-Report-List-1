<p><p>The forged steel valves market encompasses various types, including forged steel check valves, globe valves, and gate valves. Forged steel check valves prevent backflow in piping systems, ensuring unidirectional flow. Globe valves are designed for regulating fluid flow, offering precise control with a spherical body. Gate valves provide on/off control with minimal pressure drop, featuring a wedge-shaped disc. Together, these valves play crucial roles in numerous industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation, ensuring efficient and reliable operation.</p></p>
