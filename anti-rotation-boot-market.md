<p><p>The anti-rotation boot market comprises two main types: walking boots and non-walking boots. Walking boots feature a rigid sole, allowing users to safely ambulate while ensuring stability and support during recovery from lower limb injuries. Non-walking boots prioritize immobilization and protection, providing a more restrictive fit to prevent movement of the injured area. Both types serve specific needs in rehabilitation, with walking boots promoting mobility and non-walking boots focusing on rest and healing.</p></p>
