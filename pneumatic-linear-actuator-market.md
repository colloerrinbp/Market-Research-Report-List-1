<p><p>The pneumatic linear actuator market includes several types primarily categorized as pneumatic linear diaphragm actuators and pneumatic linear piston actuators. Pneumatic linear diaphragm actuators utilize a flexible diaphragm to convert air pressure into linear motion, offering precision and control in applications like valve actuation. Pneumatic linear piston actuators use a piston within a cylinder, providing higher force output suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Other types may include specialized actuators designed for specific applications, enhancing versatility in diverse industrial environments.</p></p>
