<p><strong>The Veterinarian X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinarian X-ray flat panel detector market is categorized into two main types: direct conversion and indirect conversion. Direct conversion detectors utilize materials that convert X-rays directly into electrical signals, offering high image quality and rapid processing. In contrast, indirect conversion detectors use phosphor layers to convert X-rays into light, which is then transformed into electrical signals. Both types cater to different veterinary imaging needs, with direct conversion providing superior sensitivity and resolution, while indirect conversion offers a more cost-effective solution.</p></p>
