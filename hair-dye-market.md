<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi- & Demi-Permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair dye market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dye. Temporary hair dye provides short-lived color, typically washing out after a few shampoos, making it ideal for experimentation. Semi-permanent dye offers a longer-lasting option, usually fading over several weeks but not requiring a permanent commitment. Demi-permanent hair dye provides a balance, offering richer color without the harshness of permanent dyes, providing longer-lasting results than semi-permanent while still being less damaging. Permanent hair dye offers long-lasting color that requires regrowth maintenance, making it a favorite for those seeking a lasting change.</p></p>
