<p><ul><li>Passive Heat Sink</li><li>Active Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper heat sink market is divided into two main types: passive and active heat sinks. Passive heat sinks rely on natural convection and radiation to dissipate heat without any moving parts, making them energy-efficient and low-maintenance. In contrast, active heat sinks incorporate fans or other mechanical components to enhance heat dissipation, providing greater cooling performance, especially in high-power applications. Both types play a critical role in thermal management across various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
