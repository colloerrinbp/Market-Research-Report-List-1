<p><p>The adhesive resin market is categorized into several types, each serving distinct applications. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and ease of cleanup. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding for heavy-duty applications. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond upon cooling, ideal for quick applications. Reactive adhesives cure through chemical processes, ensuring durable bonds in challenging conditions. Other types include UV-curable and pressure-sensitive adhesives, catering to specialized industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
