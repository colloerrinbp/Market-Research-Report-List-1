<p><strong>The Miniature Rocker Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SPST</li><li>SPDT</li><li>DPST</li><li>DPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature rocker switch market is segmented into several types based on their switching mechanisms. SPST (Single Pole Single Throw) switches control one circuit, enabling simple on/off functionality. SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) switches can connect to two circuits, allowing for versatile switching options. DPST (Double Pole Single Throw) switches control two circuits simultaneously, providing on/off functionality for multiple systems. DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw) switches offer dual circuit connections with two selectable paths, facilitating complex control applications in various electronic devices.</p></p>
