<p><p>The immunoprecipitation testing market is categorized into several types based on their application and target molecules. Individual Protein Immunoprecipitation isolates specific proteins from complex mixtures, enabling detailed study of protein interactions. Co-Immunoprecipitation (Co-IP) explores protein-protein interactions, facilitating the understanding of cellular mechanisms. Chromatin Immunoprecipitation (ChIP) targets DNA-protein interactions, essential for studying gene regulation. RNA Immunoprecipitation (RIP) focuses on RNA-protein interactions, aiding in the investigation of RNA biology. Each type serves distinct purposes in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
