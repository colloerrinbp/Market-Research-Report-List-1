Semi-automatic couplings streamline this process, reducing the need for manual intervention while still involving some manual steps. Automatic couplings offer the most advanced technology, enabling quick and efficient connection and disconnection without manual assistance, enhancing safety and operational efficiency in railway operations. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
